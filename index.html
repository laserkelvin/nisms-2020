The International Symposium on Molecular Spectroscopy has been a long running meeting for spectroscopists, chemists, physicists, and astronomers to meet and exchange ideas on an annual basis. Due to the unfortunate timing of Covid-19, the 75th landmark of the meeting was forced to be postponed. Among those who miss out the most are early career researchers: undergraduates, graduates, and postdoctoral fellows who now do not get the opportunity to present their research in a scientifically and socially enriching environment.
